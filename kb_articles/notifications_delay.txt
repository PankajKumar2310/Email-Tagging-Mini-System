Title: Why Notifications May Be Delayed

Common reasons for delayed desktop or mobile notifications:

- High email processing load.
- Device sleep mode.
- Notifications disabled in browser or OS settings.

Fix Checklist:
1. Ensure push notifications are enabled in Hiver.
2. Allow notifications in your browser permissions.
3. Restart the Hiver Chrome extension.
4. Update the mobile app to the latest release.
