Title: Billing and Subscription Details

Hiver billing is based on active users and selected subscription plan.

Key Billing Rules:
- Charges apply per active user seat.
- Deactivated users stop billing automatically.
- Invoices are available under Billing Settings.

Understanding an Invoice:
- Subscription period
- Number of seats
- Applied discounts
- Taxes and credits

To request invoice adjustments or refunds, contact billing support.
