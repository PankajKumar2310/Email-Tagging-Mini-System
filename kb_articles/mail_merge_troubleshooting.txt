Title: Troubleshooting Mail Merge Issues

If a Mail Merge fails or gets stuck:

Checklist:
1. Ensure your CSV contains valid email addresses.
2. Avoid duplicate or empty rows.
3. Check that Mail Merge is enabled for your inbox.
4. Large attachments may delay sending.

Fixes:
- Re-upload the CSV and preview before sending.
- Refresh the browser and retry.
- If queued for longer than 5 minutes, contact support.

Mail Merge works best for:
✓ Follow-ups  
✓ Surveys  
✓ Newsletter-style outreach
